Technical Deep Dive

A closer look at the precision and patience required for our signature processing methods.

wb_sunny

Natural Process

The cherries are sun-dried on raised African beds for 15-21 days. We rotate them every 30 minutes to ensure uniform drying. This allows the sugars from the fruit pulp to permeate the bean, resulting in a complex, fruit-forward cup with heavy body.

thermostat Target Moisture: 10.5% - 11.5%
water_drop

Fully Washed

Cherries are pulped immediately after harvest. The beans then undergo a 36-48 hour wet fermentation to break down the mucilage, followed by meticulous washing in channels. This method highlights the bean's intrinsic floral and citrus acidity.

timer Fermentation: 48 Hours Max
science

Anaerobic Fermentation

Whole cherries are sealed in oxygen-free stainless steel tanks for 72 to 120 hours. Carbon dioxide is monitored to control the pressure and temperature. This innovative method produces wild, wine-like notes and intense tropical flavors.

monitoring Pressure Controlled Environment
hive

Honey Process

A precise hybrid where the skin is removed but varying amounts of mucilage are left on the bean during drying. This balances the clarity of a washed coffee with the sweetness of a natural. We specialize in Red and Black Honey profiles.
